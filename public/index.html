Working through ENGWRIT 112 - A Primer

Why is English important to learn? Everyone speaks the language, don’t they? To get here, you’d obviously know how to read and write as well. So why 112? Why the stubborn insistence on an English course in your first year?

The answer lies somewhere outside white and black, instead preferring to nestle itself in the spectrum of color that is the human propensity to create. ENGWRIT 112 is a course that teaches you to accept your writing and actively work on improving it (with a few helpful pointers). However, the resolution of that wonderful creative expression we all have *(yes, even you, Alex)* depends on your skill with which you write.
